About me...

My name is Adriana Cottle, or Adri for short, and I am currently a student at Brigham Young University studying Informations Systems and French. Born and raised in Bellevue, Washington, I grew up participating in anything from dance classes to basketball practices. Despite being involved in so many different activities, I was able to find a passion in the arts. I was always the one to have doodles up and down the sides of my notes, and if I had a dollar for every sketchbook I’ve owned, I’d have a decent amount of money on my hands. Along with my love for drawing here and there, I also found myself immersed in the world of both competitive and performance dance, though I greatly prefer the latter, and after almost 15 years of tap dance training, I am still performing and have even taken up teaching.

Since coming to BYU, I had to search quite a bit to find a major best suited for me, and was having troubles, until I took an intro to computer programming class where I discovered I kind of like to code. A semester later, and I discovered Informations Systems and website design which combined my new interest in programming, along with my passion for the arts and the thrill of problem solving.
